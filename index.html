Bunny Minor

The minor is the smallest representative of the rabbit family. The weight of an adult often does not exceed 1.1 kg, ears-up to 6 cm. The body length of the Minorets is literally 20-25 cm.When breeding the breed, one of the main tasks was to get mini-rabbits with an easy-going character. Animals are non-aggressive and get along well with humans. The content is practically no different from the care of other breeds of dwarf rabbits.
